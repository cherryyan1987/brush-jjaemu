What is Brush Jjaemu?

Brush Jjaemu is a viral reflex game where you try to groom an orange cat using a hairbrush. The goal is simple: brush for as long as possible — but stop instantly when the cat turns.

One wrong move and you get bitten. Game over.

Why is it so addictive?

The game combines simple controls with brutal timing. Every round feels easy — until it isn't. This creates a powerful "one more try" loop that keeps players coming back.

How to play

Click or tap to brush the cat. Keep brushing to earn points. Watch closely — the moment the cat turns, stop immediately.
